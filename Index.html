About
Camouflage is when something is hidden or disguised by blending in with its surroundings. Humans feel many emotions besides happiness and sadness. But lately, many people hide their feelings or don’t express them to others. It feels like we’re choosing masks to cover our true selves. By using different emojis, this is like my personal diary, where I explore what makes me feel those emotions.
